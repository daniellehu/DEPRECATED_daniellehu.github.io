Before we start...

WARNINGS & COMMENTS ON SAFETY:

1. Batteries are dangerous! Don't leave them connected unless you're trying to fly. Charge them in a "li-po bag" to prevent them from catching on fire and burning down the building.
2. Propellers are dangerous! If your quadcopter happens to start spinning with propellers and you are not a safe distance away... you may lose your finger to that small plastic blade.
3. Everything could potentially be dangerous! Please keep common sense in mind when doing anything with Quadcopters. #1 golden rule = stay safe.

ALSO:

Feel free to ask us (Kevin and Danielle) questions anytime! We want you to have fun, but we don't know what your definition of fun is! Let us know if we bore you or if you don't understand anything, and we'll try to pivot our direction to satisfy everyone.
